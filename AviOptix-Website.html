Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about AviOptix

How does the offline functionality work?

AviOptix stores all your data locally on your device when you're offline. Once you reconnect to the internet, it automatically syncs with the cloud, ensuring you never lose any important information.

Which poultry breeds does AviOptix support?

We support all major broiler breeds (Cobb500, Ross308) and commercial layers. You can also set custom standards for other breeds you might be raising.

Can I use AviOptix on multiple devices?

Yes! With a Pro or Enterprise account, you can access your farm data from multiple devices. All changes sync automatically across your devices.

Is my data secure with AviOptix?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data. We never share your farm information with third parties without your explicit consent.

What currencies does AviOptix support?

We support multiple African currencies including TZS, KES, UGX, NGN, GHS, and ZAR. You can easily switch between currencies in the app settings.
